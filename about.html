MVMT delivers to you a Relaxed Foods Experience.

In Relaxed Foods, MVMT has created an extraordinary experience without compromising health or ethics.

From this endeavour is born a unique occasion for everyone at home to enjoy incredible flavours and textures without the addition of unhealthy chemicals or additives, so prevalent in our industry. We believe in organic, gluten-free, quality ingredients.

Our food and beverages are thoughtful, not only in their impeccable execution but also in choosing the ingredients to reflect our high moral and ethical values as they relate to all forms of oppression, the environment, health and more. And, these values are not restricted to food and beverages, which is why we choose to pay our team never less than living wages. Which is why at MVMT we try and give back by volunteering, by donating, by attending protest movements, by financing vegan educational centers and more. We believe, believe with us.

We hope you will enjoy, in the comfort of your home, our signature meals.
